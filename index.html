About

I make data
work for people.

I'm a data analyst who believes the best analysis is the kind that actually gets used. My work centers on transforming raw, complex data into clear narratives and reports that help teams make faster, smarter decisions.

With hands-on experience in Python, SQL, Power BI, and Excel, I specialize in building dashboards that communicate, cleaning data that misleads, and writing queries that answer the right questions.

I enjoy the full journey: from the messy reality of raw data to the polished insight at the end. If there's a problem that data can solve, I want to be the person solving it.
